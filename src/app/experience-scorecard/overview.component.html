Across the five core areas we looked at, most experiences in both Edge and Chrome were rated as highly usable and familiar. Chrome received slightly higher ratings overall, but generally by very small margins.
Top Issues
  1. Tabs:Moving tabs between and within windows in Edge is more difficult than in Chrome.
  2. Newsfeed on NTP:Newsfeed on NTP has low usefulness for most users.
  3. Apps on the NTP:App installation links on the NTP create usability problems for new users and funnel them out of Edge.
  4. Favorites:Favorites in Edge are usable, but less desirable than bookmarks in Chrome.
  5. Personality:Edge’s app icon and layout are less desirable than Chrome’s.
